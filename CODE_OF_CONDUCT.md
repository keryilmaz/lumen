# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project sometimes attracts people in difficult situations — patients facing serious diagnoses, family members trying to understand what's happening, clinicians thinking about how to help. Discussions, issues, and PRs should reflect that.

We pledge to make participation in Lumen a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users
- Showing empathy toward other community members — especially those discussing personal medical situations

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission (especially health-related)
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer via [GitHub's report-abuse flow](https://docs.github.com/en/communities/maintaining-your-safety-on-github/reporting-abuse-or-spam) or by opening a private security advisory.
